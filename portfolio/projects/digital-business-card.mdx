A digital platform designed to simplify the creation, management, and exchange of professional business cards.
Users can create their own digital cards, save them, and share them with others. The project integrates LinkedIn for networking and professional collaboration.

**Technologies Used**

- **Frontend**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Hono.js, Prisma, MongoDB
- **Authentication**: Google OAuth
- **Deployment**: Vercel

**Key Features**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Linkedin Integration">
    Seamlessly connects users' LinkedIn profiles with digital cards, enabling
    instant access to professional information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customizable Digital Cards">
    Users can personalize their digital business cards with logos, contact
    details, and social media links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-time Sharing">
    Share your card via email, QR code, or direct link to anyone, regardless of
    whether they have the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analytics Dashboard">
    Track how often your business card is shared, viewed, and interacted with.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Challenges Overcome**

- **LinkedIn API Integration**: Overcame rate-limiting challenges and integrated LinkedIn's OAuth flow securely.
- **Cross-platform Compatibility**: Ensured that the platform is responsive and optimized for mobile devices.
